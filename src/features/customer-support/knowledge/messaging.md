# Messaging Guide

ValClassifieds includes built-in messaging so buyers and sellers can talk safely
without sharing personal contact details.

## Starting a conversation
- Open a listing and tap **Message** to contact the seller.
- Your first message is sent to the seller's ValClassifieds inbox.

## Finding your messages
- Open the **Messages** icon from the menu or header.
- Conversations are listed most-recent first.
- Unread messages are marked.

## Tips for good conversations
- Be polite and specific.
- Ask about condition, availability, and meeting location.
- Keep all arrangements in the chat so there is a record.

## Privacy
- Do not post phone numbers, emails, addresses, or payment links in messages.
- ValClassifieds may review messages only when investigating abuse or safety reports.

## Problems with a conversation
If a user is abusive, spammy, or suspicious, use the **Block** or **Report** option
in the conversation.
