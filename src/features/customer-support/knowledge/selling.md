# Selling Guide

Selling on ValClassifieds is free and easy. This guide walks you through it.

## Creating a listing
1. Tap **Post** (or **Create Listing**) from the menu.
2. Choose the right category for your item.
3. Add a clear title and an honest description.
4. Set a fair price.
5. Upload good-quality photos.
6. Add your location so nearby buyers can find you.
7. Publish the listing.

## Writing a great description
- Be honest about the condition (new, like new, used, for parts).
- Mention age, brand, size, and any defects.
- Answer buyer questions promptly.

## Photos
- Use natural lighting and a clean background.
- Show the item from multiple angles.
- Include close-ups of any damage.

## Managing your listings
- Go to **Dashboard → My Listings** to edit or delete a listing.
- Mark an item as **Sold** once it's no longer available.
- You can promote a listing to get more visibility (see Marketplace Rules).

## Pricing
You set your own price. Compare similar active listings to price competitively.
ValClassifieds does not set or negotiate prices for you.

## Safety
Meet buyers in public places and complete the exchange in person when possible.
See the Safety Tips guide.
