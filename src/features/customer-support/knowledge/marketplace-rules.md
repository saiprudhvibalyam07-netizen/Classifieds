# Marketplace Rules

These rules keep ValClassifieds safe and useful for everyone.

## Allowed listings
- Physical items you own and are allowed to sell.
- Honest descriptions and real photos of the actual item.

## Not allowed
- Illegal goods, weapons, drugs, or regulated items.
- Counterfeit or stolen items.
- Adult content, hate, or harmful material.
- Tickets, animals/pets (where prohibited), or anything requiring a license you don't
  have.
- Duplicate or spam listings.

## Behavior
- Be respectful in messages and listings.
- No harassment, discrimination, or threats.
- No attempts to move deals off-platform to evade safety.

## Pricing and promotions
- You set your own prices.
- Promoted listings get extra visibility but must still follow the rules.
- Do not manipulate prices or post fake "sold" activity.

## Enforcement
- Listings that break the rules are removed.
- Repeat or serious violations can lead to account suspension.
- Use **Report** to flag rule-breaking content.

ValClassifieds reserves the right to remove content and suspend accounts to protect
the community.
