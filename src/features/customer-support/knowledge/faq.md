# Frequently Asked Questions

**Is ValClassifieds free to use?**
Posting and browsing listings is free. Buyers and sellers arrange payment directly.

**Do I need an account to browse?**
You can browse and search without an account, but you need to sign up to message
sellers or post listings.

**How do I contact a seller?**
Open the listing and tap **Message**.

**How do I edit or delete my listing?**
Go to **Dashboard → My Listings**, then choose edit or delete.

**Can ValClassifieds refund a payment?**
ValClassifieds does not process payments, so it cannot issue refunds. Any payment
agreement is between the buyer and seller.

**How do I report a suspicious listing or user?**
Use the **Report** option on the listing or in the conversation.

**I forgot my password.**
Use **Forgot password** on the login page to receive a reset link.

**Is my information private?**
ValClassifieds does not ask for passwords or payment details in messages. See the
Account Help and Safety Tips guides.

**Can the assistant access my account or messages?**
No. This assistant can only provide guidance from the support documentation. It cannot
view accounts, listings, messages, or payments.
