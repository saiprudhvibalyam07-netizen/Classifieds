# Account Help

Your ValClassifieds account lets you post listings, message sellers, and save favorites.

## Creating an account
- Tap **Sign up** and register with your email.
- Verify your email address when prompted.
- Complete your profile (name, location) to build trust with buyers and sellers.

## Signing in
- Tap **Log in** and enter your email and password.
- If you forgot your password, use **Forgot password** to receive a reset link.

## Managing your account
- Open **Profile** to update your name, photo, and location.
- Open **Dashboard** to manage your listings, favorites, and messages.

## Favorites
- Tap the heart on any listing to save it to **Favorites**.
- View and remove favorites from the **Favorites** page.

## Deleting or disabling your account
Account deletion is handled by ValClassifieds support. If you need help, contact
support from this chat or via the help options in your profile.

## Security
- Use a strong, unique password.
- Never share your password with anyone.
- ValClassifieds will never ask for your password in a message.
