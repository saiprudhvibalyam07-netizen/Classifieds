# Buying Guide

Welcome to buying on ValClassifieds! This guide explains how to find and purchase
items safely.

## Finding items
- Use the search bar at the top of any page to look for keywords (e.g. "bike", "sofa").
- Browse by category from the home page or the categories menu.
- Use filters (price, location, condition) to narrow results.
- Open a listing to see the full description, photos, price, and seller location.

## Contacting a seller
- On a listing page, use the **Message** button to chat with the seller.
- Ask questions about condition, availability, and pickup before agreeing to buy.
- All communication stays inside ValClassifieds messaging.

## Staying safe
- Meet in a public place for in-person exchanges.
- Inspect the item before paying.
- Do not share personal financial information in messages.
- See the Safety Tips guide for more.

## Payments
ValClassifieds does not process payments. Buyers and sellers arrange payment
directly. Never pay before you have seen the item, and be cautious of unusual
payment requests.

## If something goes wrong
ValClassifieds cannot guarantee transactions between users. If you suspect a scam,
stop communicating and report the listing using the report option on the listing page.
