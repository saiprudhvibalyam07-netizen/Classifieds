# Safety Tips

Your safety is the top priority when buying and selling on ValClassifieds.

## Meeting in person
- Meet in busy public places (malls, cafés, transit stations).
- Bring a friend if possible.
- Meet during daylight hours.

## Inspecting items
- Check the item carefully before paying.
- Test electronics and confirm condition matches the description.

## Payments
- ValClassifieds does not handle payments.
- Avoid wire transfers, gift cards, or advance payments for items you haven't seen.
- Be cautious of overpayment or "shipping agent" scams.

## Personal information
- Keep your address, phone number, and financial details private until you trust the
  other person.
- Don't share passwords, OTPs, or bank details in messages.

## Recognizing scams
- Prices that are too good to be true.
- Sellers who refuse to meet or show the item.
- Requests to pay outside ValClassifieds.
- Pressure to act quickly.

## Reporting
If something feels wrong, stop and use the **Report** option on the listing or in the
conversation. You can also tell us here in support.
