# Listing Guide

A listing is a single post that describes an item you want to sell.

## What every listing should have
- A descriptive title
- An accurate category
- A clear description (condition, details, pickup location)
- At least one photo
- A price

## Listing status
- **Active** — visible to buyers and searchable.
- **Sold** — hidden from search once the item is sold.
- **Draft / Hidden** — not yet published or paused.

## Editing a listing
1. Open **Dashboard → My Listings**.
2. Select the listing and choose **Edit**.
3. Save your changes; they appear immediately.

## Deleting a listing
Remove a listing from **Dashboard → My Listings** when you no longer need it.
Deleted listings are removed from search.

## Improving visibility
- Use a specific title with the item name and key detail (e.g. "iPhone 12, 128GB, blue").
- Add multiple photos.
- Keep the price realistic.
- Consider promoting the listing (see Marketplace Rules).

## Reporting a listing
If you see a suspicious or rule-breaking listing, open it and use the **Report**
option. Our team reviews reports.
