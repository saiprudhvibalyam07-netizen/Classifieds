# Navigation Help

New to ValClassifieds? Here is how to get around.

## Home page
- See featured and recent listings.
- Use the search bar and category menu to explore.

## Top menu / header
- **Search** — find items by keyword.
- **Categories** — browse by category.
- **Messages** — open your conversations.
- **Post / Create Listing** — start a new listing.
- **Dashboard** — manage your account, listings, and favorites.
- **Profile** — view and edit your profile.

## Listing page
- View photos, description, price, location, and seller info.
- Tap **Message** to contact the seller.
- Tap the heart to save to **Favorites**.

## Dashboard
- **My Listings** — create, edit, or delete your listings.
- **Favorites** — items you saved.
- **Messages** — your conversations.

## Mobile
The site is mobile-friendly. The menu collapses into a hamburger icon on small
screens; the floating support button (this chat) is always available at the bottom
right.
